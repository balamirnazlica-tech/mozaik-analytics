Executive Summary & Key Takeaways
✓ Strengths

Strong ROAS across both platforms (7.75x–13.24x). Organic Search and Direct channels drive 60%+ of revenue. Eames Tekli Koltuk alone accounts for 26.4% of total product revenue. Google Ads efficiency improved during Katapult management period.
⚠ Areas for Improvement

Cart abandonment rate of 90.7% needs urgent attention. Paid Social generates only 0.4% of revenue despite 16.6% of traffic. Feb 2026 purchase volume dropped 47.89%. The Aeron chair dominates views (19%) but doesn't appear in top revenue — potential conversion gap.
