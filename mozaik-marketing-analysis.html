Key Patterns & Insights

1. Extreme Vendor Concentration

High Risk

Doğan Burda alone accounts for 36.7% of all marketing spend (₺3.27M). Combined with Elmalma (₺1.95M), just two vendors consume 58.6% of the total budget. This creates significant dependency risk — a price increase from either vendor would heavily impact the budget.

Benchmark

The remaining 37 line items share just ₺3.69M between them. The top 5 vendors account for 76.2% of all spend.

2. Offline Dominance (92.3%)

Strategic

Combining Offline (60.3%) and Agency (32%) — which primarily supports offline channels — over 92% of spend goes to traditional/offline marketing. Digital (Google, Facebook, tools) is only 7.7% at ₺689K.

Opportunity

Magazine advertising alone (₺3.94M) is 5.7x larger than all digital advertising combined (₺689K). Modern benchmarks suggest at least 30-50% digital allocation for most consumer brands.

3. Strong Seasonal Spikes

Pattern

Spending peaks notably in May (₺928K), July (₺912K), November (₺924K), and December (₺1.38M). The December spike is largely driven by PR/gifting expenses (chocolates, food gifts) and year-end magazine buys.

Budget Planning

The quietest months are January (₺363K) and August (₺402K) — roughly half the peak months. This volatility makes budget forecasting harder.

4. Late-Year Digital Ramp-Up

Trend

Facebook Ads went from ₺0 for 8 months straight (Jan–Aug) to suddenly ₺206K in the final 4 months. Google Ads also doubled from Oct onward. This suggests either a strategic shift toward digital or a late push to spend remaining budget.

Positive

The Performance Marketing line (Katapult, ₺180K) also started only in Q4 — indicating an emerging focus on measurable results.

5. PR/Gifting is a Hidden Cost Center

Watch

Combining PR, PR-Photography, Beverage, and Catering categories totals ₺1.30M (14.6% of budget). These are mostly event and gifting costs (chocolates from Divan/240 Derece, wine from Adco Şarap, wooden boxes). Much of this is concentrated in July and December.

Flag

ADCO ŞARAP appears twice — once under Beverage (₺178K) and once under PR (₺145K) — totaling ₺323K from a single wine supplier. This deserves scrutiny for ROI.

6. Agency Fee Structure Shift

Pattern

Elmalma (website agency) was at a flat ₺175K/month through September, then dropped to ₺84K/month from October — a 52% reduction. This may indicate a contract renegotiation, scope change, or agency transition.

New Additions

Meanwhile, Bir İki Üç (Creative, ₺718K) and Katapult (Performance, ₺180K) were added in Q3/Q4, suggesting the agency roster is being diversified and specialized.
